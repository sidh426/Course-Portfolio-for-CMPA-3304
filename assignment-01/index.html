My Top 5 Movies

  1. The Dark Knight

    A superhero movie about Batman trying to stop the Joker from causing chaos in Gotham City.

  2. Interstellar

    A science fiction film about astronauts who travel through a wormhole in space to find a new home for humanity.

  3. The Shawshank Redemption

    A story about a man who is sent to prison for a crime he did not commit and his journey to find freedom.

  4. Inception

    A thriller about a thief who enters people's dreams to steal secrets from their subconscious mind.

  5. Forrest Gump

    A film that follows the life of a man from Alabama who accidentally becomes part of many major historical events.
